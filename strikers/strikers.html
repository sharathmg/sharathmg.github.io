STRIKERS

2014 / 2015

STRIKERS is a data visualization of the scorers table and goal stats of the top football-soccer leagues in the world.
The bubble visualization is a representation of the scorers stats who have more than 4-5 goals.
The data presented on the right is based on the total scoring stats of each of the leagues.
To see the total scoring stats and name for a player just hover the bubbles.
